Little Rock 9

How racist could highschoolers be?

1957

Three years after Brown v. Board of Education was decided, nine black students were chosen based on grades and behavior to be integrated into the white Little Rock Central High School. The Arkansas governor then sent in the National guard to preserve the peace, by denying the nine students entry. President Eisenhower overturned these orders and told the national guard to escort the nine students in. This was an early step of fully integrating high schools around the nation.
